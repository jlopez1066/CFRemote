A Bundle & Save Package allows you to get a great deal on any frame collection, not just the ones on sale. For one great price, you’ll get a frame, any two standard mats and Conservation Clear® glass. Visit the store to see a Certified Framing Expert for more information.

HOW LONG WILL IT TAKE TO RECEIVE MY COMPLETED ORDER?
Approximately 14 days, but we do offer seven-day expedited service for an additional charge.

WHAT IF I NEED TO GET SOMETHING FRAMED AT THE LAST MINUTE?
Ask any Certified Framing Expert about Custom Frame Express. We’ll pair an off-the-shelf frame (or a frame you already own) with a custom mat and glazing to give you some of the benefits of a custom frame—but much faster. In most cases, it takes only 24 hours.

HOW IS MY ARTWORK INSERTED INTO THE FRAME?
It depends on the artwork, but we offer several mounting methods, from dry mount to stitched mount and preservation-grade mount.

Sorry, not at this time.

CAN I PURCHASE A MAT OR GLASS WITHOUT PURCHASING A FRAME?
You certainly can! Many of our customers want to refresh a previously framed item, and this is a great solution. We can even add any mat or glazing to an off-the-shelf frame. Ask one of our Certified Framing Experts about Custom Frame Express.

WHAT IS A GLAZING?
A glazing is any kind of material on the outside of the frame used to protect the artwork. We offer glass, acrylic and Print Guard. Glass or acrylic are recommended for custom frames because they protect the artwork from pollution and harmful UV light. Print Guard is a water-resistant, scratch-resistant laminate glazing for flat, printed art.

HOW WIDE SHOULD MY MAT BE?
It depends on the size of your art and the frame you choose. However, our Certified Framing Experts think that wider mats create a better stage—so to speak—for most artwork.

I CANNOT SEE MY ART BECAUSE THE GLASS IS SO REFLECTIVE! IS THERE A NON-GLARE GLAZING AVAILABLE?
Our Conservation Masterpiece® glass and acrylic glazings provide reduced reflections, amazing clarity and 99% UV protection.

ARE MY MATERIALS TOO THICK FOR THE PICTURE FRAME?
Probably not. After all, these are custom frames and shadow boxes! We can add space to the back of a frame or layer frames to build enough depth for dimensional objects.

Preservation Techniques

THE AREA I WANT TO HANG MY FRAME IN HAS DIRECT SUNLIGHT. IS THERE GLASS I CAN USE TO PROTECT THE ART?
We offer four different glazing options, and all of them provide 99% UV protection.

WHAT KIND OF TAPE DO YOU USE TO ATTACH MY ART TO THE MAT?
Because a preservation mount (placing the art on a board to go into the frame) needs to be removable and reversible, we use archival photo corners.

HOW DO YOU AVOID THE COLLECTION OF MOISTURE BETWEEN MY ART AND THE GLAZING?
In many cases, moisture is present in the artwork itself, so only proper spacing will prevent it from sticking to the glass. Our Certified Framing Experts use mats and/or spacers to create a gap and allow for airflow between the art and the glazing.

HOW DO YOU ENSURE THAT MY ARTWORK IS SAFE FROM THE EFFECTS OF ACID OVER TIME?
All of our custom frames are preservation-quality and acid-free. We also use aluminum barrier tape to line the inside of your frame, preventing any acid from the wooden frame from migrating to the artwork.
